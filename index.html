About Us

Where, when, why, how Noir was formed

Noir Project is a project that is started by 5 college musician with the same goal, have fun. Noir formed in 2019 when a college event motivates them to create a band. Noir offers a wide range of genre like pop, jazz, dangdut, rock and reggae. Noir ensures your every order crafted to fits your needs, wether it’s a wedding, birthday, art show, everything! We’re here to have fun!
